Irakal (Preys) Short Film - WIP

IRAKAL is a personal short film project I began developing a couple of years ago while I was in India. The key inspiration came from a decommissioned amphitheater I visited with friends while backpacking. I developed a story around the location, planned shots, created storyboards, and surveyed the location to build a VIRTUAL environment for VIRTUAL PRODUCTION. Now I'm also using some Gen AI tools in the pipeline!
