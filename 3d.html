Irakal (Preys) Short Film - WIP

IRAKAL is a personal short film project I began developing a couple of years ago while I was in India. The key inspiration came from a decommissioned amphitheater I visited with friends while backpacking. I developed a story around the location, planned shots, created storyboards, and surveyed the location to build a VIRTUAL environment. Now I'm also using some Gen AI tools to animate the wolf!

Cochin Carnival 2022 Teaser for Kerala Tourism

As creative director for Cochin Carnival 2022—Kochi’s iconic New Year festival drawing over 500,000 attendees—I designed the 60-foot “Papanji” effigy, the festival’s centerpiece. Burned at midnight on New Year’s Eve, Papanji was imagined welcoming foreign visitors while crushing a coronavirus cell, inspired by the post-pandemic return of tourism and the myth of Vamana and Mahabali. I also created Franco, the event mascot—a hermit crab with a discarded sanitizer cap as its shell—to raise awareness about plastic pollution along Fort Kochi’s shores. The animated promotional video was fully produced in Blender, where I handled concept, modeling, stylized rendering, lighting, and sound, bringing the festival’s story to life in motion.
