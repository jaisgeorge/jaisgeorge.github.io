ABOUT

Jais grew up in Kerala, India, making art and devouring stories — from local literature and cinema to adventure tales from the West. After graduating as a Mechanical Engineer, he worked as a Research Engineer for two years before stepping into cinema. He spent another two years in Indian films as an Assistant Director, Story Artist, and 2nd AC. In this time, he also served as the Creative Director for a major Indian festival, before flying to Texas for graduate studies.

Currently in Texas, he’s exploring 3D, virtual production, and generative AI for filmmaking and theater. His creative path spans a variety of media, and these diverse experiences continuously inform and enrich his work. Jais thrives in creative, technical, and collaborative environments. Driven by a sense of adventure he picked up from his early days, he's constantly creating, unafraid to take risks, and enjoys making things happen. Always ready for any unexpected journey.
