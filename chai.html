Resume Spot Chai?

When I moved from my home in Kerala, South India, to Texas, it was my first journey abroad, and I set off with only a backpack and a tent. My stop along the way was Japan. For several weeks, I slept in places from parks to an abandoned building with a view of Mt. Fuji to a cozy Japanese home.

A week before that trip, I was an Assistant Director and Story Artist in Indian Cinema, and before that, a Research Engineer. My creative path spans traditional art and graphic design, 3D animation, storyboarding, video production, live-action cinema and virtual production, theater, motion capture, 3D printing, interactive projections, installations, and most recently, Generative AI. I continue to explore a wide spectrum of creative practices, and these diverse skill sets inform and enrich my work. I graduated in 2020 as a Mechanical Engineer and spent two years as a Research Engineer—a role I genuinely enjoyed - it was very much visual too! Leaving that career behind for storytelling was not an easy decision. Three years on, I'm proud of the creative avenues I have explored. In short, I enjoy making things, making things happen and don't worry too much about risks - I’ve trusted a one-person tent more than I probably should, and it’s served me well. And I'm always ready for an unexpected journey. Chase the dream, not the competition.

Now tell me your story. I'll be a good listener!
