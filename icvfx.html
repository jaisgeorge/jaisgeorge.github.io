VIRTUAL PRODUCTION SUMMER INDUSTRY COURSE 2024

During summer 2024, I served as a teaching assistant and student technician for Texas A&M's Virtual Production Summer Industry Course. My role involved assisting a group of 16 graduate and undergraduate students in creating three short films over the course of the summer. The films were all shot entirely in a virtual production volume at Stray Vista Studios in Austin over a cumulative two weeks. Before this, the students created digital environments and pre-visualisations in Unreal Engine over the course of eight weeks.
